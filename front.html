The Mission Statement

The Wright Cut offers an experience that is unparalleled to its competitors, providing employment opportunities to both male and female professionals and student cosmetologists. The facility provides an atmosphere that creates quality, grade-a service that will bring comfort and appreciation to its customers. The Wright Cut also enhances the academic, socialistic, and economic growth of the entire Rutgers Community. It is a place of fellowship for mentoring and guidance, between the customer and the service provider.
